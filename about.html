What About Andy?

I am a undergraduate student currently studying Computer Science at the University of Louisville. While Computer Science is my major focus, Mathematics has always been one of my biggest strengths, which is why I am pursuing it as a minor.

I have been learning a lot of programming languages, but my most proficient ones as of now are Java, C/C++ and Python. If you came from my resume and need a refresher, or if you are visiting the page for the first time, my resume can be found here.

During 2024, I was a part of the University of Louisville's Speed ACM Game Development Club, which was a fun and unique experience I had working with very talented people!

Not Just Software

Besides programming though, I also love drawing and playing music in my free time. Expressing myself through creative means relaxes me, and gives me a feeling of growth outside of school and work.

Working closely with such a diverse group of people and being so driven for improvement shaped who I am today. Although I don't play in a band for UofL, I still occasionally try to learn how to play the bass guitar.

A lot of my previous projects are game-related, which is because I love to play them. When I can't seem to find the inspiration to draw, I often look to games to find it.
